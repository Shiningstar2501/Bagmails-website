How Bagmails Works

Bagmails connects senders who want to ship items with travelers already heading in that direction—saving time and money for both.

1. Post Your Delivery

Senders add their package info—destination, size, and reward.

2. Traveler Accepts

A traveler on the same route picks the request and contacts the sender.

3. Confirm & Deliver

The package is delivered in person, and the traveler gets rewarded.
